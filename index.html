Xuelian Cheng

I am currently a PhD candidate in the Department of Electrical and Computer Systems Engineering(ECSE), Monash University. working with A/Prof. Zongyuan Ge, Dr. Mehrtash Harandi, Prof. Tom Drummond. My research interests include visual geometry and low-level vision tasks, i.e., depth estimation; AutoML, i.e., Neural Architecture Search(NAS); medical imaging and open-set senerios.
